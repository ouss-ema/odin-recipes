Spaghetti Carbonara

Home

Description

A creamy Italian pasta made with eggs, cheese, and crispy bacon. Rich, simple, and very satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Bacon (or turkey bacon)
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Cook bacon until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs with grated cheese.
  4. Combine pasta with bacon.
  5. Remove from heat, add egg mixture and stir quickly.
  6. Season with pepper and serve.
