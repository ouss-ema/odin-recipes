Chocolate Mug Cake

Home

Description

A fast and easy single-serving chocolate cake made in a mug—perfect for a quick dessert craving.

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a mug..
  2. Stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave for 1–2 minutes.
  4. Let cool slightly and enjoy.
