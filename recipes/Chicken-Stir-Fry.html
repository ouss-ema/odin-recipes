Chicken Stir Fry

Home

Description

A quick and healthy dish with tender chicken and colorful vegetables, cooked in a flavorful sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast (cut pieces)
  • Mixed vegetables (pepper, carrot, broccoli)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Cook chicken until golden.
  3. Add garlic and vegetables.
  4. Stir fry for 5–7 minutes.
  5. Add soy sauce and mix well.
  6. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
